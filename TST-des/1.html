The Shade Store difference

We don't sell window coverings.
We shape the light in your home.

We started in 2018 because we'd seen enough rushed installs and pushy showrooms. Every measure is done by us. Every blind, curtain and shutter is fitted by our own team. There are no commissioned reps and no third parties between you and the workshop.

  • No commission sales

    You're advised, not upsold. Same answer whether you spend $800 or $80,000.

  • No subcontractors

    Measure and install by the same small team — every job, every time.

  • Climate-aware fabrics

  • One-year warranty

    Backed by responsive after-sales — a phone call, not a ticket queue.

How it works

Three steps. No surprises.

  1. 01

    Consult

    In your home, or over coffee.

    Free, no obligation. We measure every window, talk through fabric and finish, and explain what works for your light, your privacy and your budget.

  2. 02

    Design & install

    Made to your window. Fitted by us.

    Custom-made to your measurements, then installed by the same team that quoted the job. Most installs take a single, tidy visit.

  3. 03

    Aftercare

    A year's warranty. A direct line.

    Anything not right in the first twelve months — and we'll come back. After that, you've still got our number. We answer it.
